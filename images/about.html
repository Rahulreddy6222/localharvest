About Us

Welcome to LocalHarvest! I am Ettedi Rahul Adithya Reddy and I am extremely passionate about healthy living and sustainable food practices. I am committed to making a positive impact on the world through our business. Thank you for choosing our organic food products — we look forward to serving you soon.

Our Story

LocalHarvest began with a simple idea — to bring transparent, farm-fresh produce from local growers directly to your table. We work closely with small farmers, avoid harmful chemicals, and focus on sustainable farming methods that protect soil and community livelihoods.

What we do

  • Handpicked seasonal fruits & vegetables
  • Farm-sourced grains, pulses & nuts
  • Cold-pressed oils and traditional masalas
  • Packaging with minimal environmental impact

Our promise

Quality, transparency and fair pricing. We test and verify our produce, and we ensure growers receive fair value for their harvests.
